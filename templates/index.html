Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rules

The object of Rock, Paper, Scissors is to choose an item that beats your opponent's item. The winning combinations are:

  • Rock blunts scissors
  • Paper wraps rock
  • Scissors cut paper

How to play

Player 1 enters their choice of item into the browser after the slash for the website address, then player two enters a slash followed by their choice

Example: http://localhost:5000/rock/scissors

Press enter and the winner will be displayed. In the above example, Player 1 would win.
